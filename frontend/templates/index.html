Virtual Office in the Cloud

Although most of the documents we deal with nowadays are digital, the way we manage them is outdated. This is especially true for office and government systems. Documents are important in business and government because they record the development of thoughts, laws, and decisions as historical records. However, paper-based hard copies are vulnerable to theft, destruction, and loss, while digital documents are susceptible to cybercrimes.
